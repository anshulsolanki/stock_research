Strategy Guide

  • Climax Churn (Purple X): High volume (>2x Avg) with small body at highs. Often indicates "smart money" selling into strength.
  • Distribution (Maroon Dot): High volume (>Avg) on down days. Indicates institutional exiting.
  • Buying Exhaustion (Red ▼): Price makes Higher Highs while Volume makes Lower Highs. Demand drying up.
  • Selling Exhaustion (Green ▲): Price makes Lower Lows while Volume makes Lower Lows. Supply drying up.
